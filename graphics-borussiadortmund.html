Borussia Dortmund: A history in black and yellow

My friend, Terry Duffelen, wrote a book about his favourite German football team, Borussia Dortmund in 2021. He asked me to design the book from the outside cover to the typography and graphics on each new chapter page.

The front cover was coloured yellow and black to match the colours of the Borussia Dortmund kit, and all of the graphics were designed in Adobe Illustrator. Page layouts were created in Adobe In Design.
