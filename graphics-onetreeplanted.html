One Tree Planted

While undertaking an Ara Visual Design course, I was asked to design a one-page flyer to promote One Tree Planted. This is an organisation that encourages businesses to invest in the planting of trees where forest fires have occurred.

The flyer was designed to be printed on both sides of an A4 sheet of paper, folded into three parts.
